About

proStoria is a technology company incorporated in 2016 by Malene and Gordon, two experienced entrepreneurs from Denmark. They formed proStoria to develop the best DevOps tool for production test data. proStoria is striving to make a DevOps tool not only for businesses, but also for individuals, such as freelance developers, and non-profit organizations. We have branched proStoria into two versions; an enterprise version and a free community version, both based on the same engine.
